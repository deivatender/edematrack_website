Edematrack Story

EdemaTrack: Simplifying Lymphedema Measurement

EdemaTrack was born from a need for a user-friendly, cost-effective, and secure measuring software solution for lymphedema therapists.
With 25 years of experience, our founder sought to overcome the limitations of existing systems and partnered with applications engineer Yanni Sporidis to create EdemaTrack.
Our software is designed exclusively for lymphedema therapists and clinicians, informed by decades of practical experience. It's fully HIPAA compliant and cloud-based for data security. We value input from clinicians and continuously enhance EdemaTrack to align with your evolving needs.

Key Features:

  • User-friendly and intuitive
  • Progress-tracking graphics
  • Embedded Made-to-Measure forms
  • Affordable for clinics of all sizes
  • HIPAA compliant and secure in the cloud

Edematrack Team

John Jordi

Clinical Manager at Juzo

John Jordi, BS, PTA, CLT-LANA, CI, ASTYM

With over three decades of experience, I have been a dedicated Physical Therapist Assistant (PTA) since 1993. My journey in lymphedema care began in 1999 when I obtained my initial certification. Since then, my focus has remained steadfast on providing comprehensive lymphedema treatment and education.

Throughout my career, I have had the privilege of treating patients across all stages of lymphedema, including the implementation and management of Stage 3 inpatient programs. My commitment to education has led me to instruct fellow clinicians and students through organizations like ACOLS, Klose Training, and as adjunct faculty at the University of Tennessee at Chattanooga.

Collaborating with peers, I have co-authored and published research articles on lymphedema, and I have actively participated in beta testing new patient measurement products in the field. Despite the array of measurement devices available, my unwavering dedication to precision has led me to prefer manual measurements for their unmatched accuracy.
Driven by the desire to address my own clinical needs and assist fellow therapists in the long-term management of lymphedema patients, EdemaTrack was created.
